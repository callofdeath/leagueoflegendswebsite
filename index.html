Welkom bij het spel uitleg van League of Legends

In league of Legends heb je afgesproken rollen op de map die verdeeld zijn over 3 lijnen

Er is ook een non-competetieve omgeving

Genaamd 'Aram'. Dat is een 1 lane map waarbij een 5v5 wordt gehouden

Er zijn momenteel 150verschillende champions beschikbaar om te spelen
Er zijn meerdere regionale servers

Hier zijn ze bij elkaar gezet met het % van aantal spelers dat er spelen

Er is een jaarlijks event waarbij de beste teams over de hele wereld meedoen voor de wereldtitel

Iron tier

These players are the most newest to the game and are trying to learn to play the game

Bronze tier

These players are still trying to learn the game but are improving

Silver tier

These players have found a champion they like and are likely playing it a lot

Gold tier

These players are understanding the fundamentals of the game but aren't quite good at being consious of what to do

Platinum tier

These players are getting into the top 10% of the server, they are close to knowing all fundamentals and are mechanicly good enough to come by

Diamond tier

Good job! You have reached top1% in the server. These players know the fundamentals but still need to improve a lot to go to the top!

Master tier

These players know all basic fundamentals and are mechanicly really good.

Grandmaster tier

These players should go outside and see some sun. They know (almost) everything in the game there is to know and are mechanicly godlike compared to 'lower' tiers such as gold or lower

Challenger tier

These players should all consider to go pro. They are insanely good at this game and can't be compared to any other tiers
